Frequently Asked Questions

When will my profile be approved?

Most profiles are reviewed within 24–48 hours after submission. You'll receive an email notification.

Can employers see my name or current company?

No. All identifying information is hidden until you accept a connection request from a specific employer.

How do I apply for a role?

Browse Search Roles, click Apply Confidentially, and your anonymous profile will be forwarded to the employer.

Is ExecBoard MX free for candidates?

Yes. ExecBoard MX is completely free for executive candidates. Employers pay per posting.
