Chief Technology Officer

FinTech Global MX

$2,500,000 - $3,000,000 MXN + Equity • Posted 2 days ago

Company Overview

FinTech Global MX is a leading provider of innovative financial infrastructure. We are on a mission to democratize access to global financial markets in LATAM, starting primarily in Mexico. Our team is growing rapidly, and we are looking for an experienced technical leader to guide our engineering organization into its next phase of hyper-growth.

Role Description

As the Chief Technology Officer (CTO), you will be a key member of the executive team. You will be responsible for the overarching technology strategy, driving the execution of our technical roadmap, and managing a world-class engineering team based across Mexico. You will ensure our technical architecture is scalable, secure, and aligned with our strategic business objectives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Define and execute the long-term technical vision and roadmap.
  • Lead, mentor, and scale the engineering, data, and IT teams (currently 150+ engineers in CDMX, Monterrey, and Guadalajara).
  • Partner closely with Product to deliver high-quality features rapidly.
  • Ensure adherence to security, compliance (CNBV standards), and regulatory standards.
  • Represent the company's technical vision to investors, partners, and the public.

Requirements

  • 15+ years of software engineering experience with at least 5 years in a senior leadership role (VP or C-level) in Mexico or LATAM.
  • Proven track record of scaling high-performing engineering teams.
  • Deep technical background in cloud architecture, distributed systems, and modern data practices.
  • Experience in the FinTech or regulated industries is highly preferred.
  • Excellent communication and cross-functional collaboration skills in fluent Spanish and English.
