About this Role

FinTech Global MX is seeking an exceptional Chief Technology Officer to lead the company's engineering and product technology strategy as we scale operations across Latin America. This is a pivotal leadership role at a well-funded, high-growth FinTech.

Reporting directly to the CEO, you will own the full technology vision, build and manage a world-class engineering team, and drive technical excellence across our core payments infrastructure, mobile platforms, and regulatory compliance systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Define and execute the technology roadmap aligned with business strategy and growth objectives
  • Lead and grow a distributed engineering team of 80+ across CDMX, Monterrey, and remote
  • Oversee architecture of core payments platform, ensuring CNBV and PCI-DSS compliance
  • Drive the migration from legacy infrastructure to cloud-native microservices architecture
  • Partner with board members and investors on technology strategy and capital allocation
  • Own cybersecurity posture and data governance at enterprise scale

Candidate Profile

  • 15+ years of progressive technology leadership, with 5+ years in a senior executive or CTO role
  • Deep expertise in FinTech, payments infrastructure, or regulated financial technology
  • Proven track record scaling engineering organizations from 30 to 100+ people
  • Strong cloud architecture skills (AWS preferred), microservices, and DevSecOps
  • Bilingual Spanish/English required; experience with LATAM markets strongly preferred
